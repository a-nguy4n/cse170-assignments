What are good coffee shop places in San Diego to study and work?

Here are some recommendations: Em's Coffeehouse in Mira Mesa as it has outlets and wifi,
but computer work up to 3 hours. Forum Coffeehouse includes seating and wifi. Moniker Outpost
at Liberty Station or North Park also is a good environment to work and get coffee!

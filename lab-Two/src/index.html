Homemade Sliders

Instructions

  1. Use Hawaiian breadrolls and cut them in half.
  2. *Optional: Add butter and garlic onto the breadrolls for more flavor!
  3. Season the beef patties to your liking.
  4. Cook the beef patties on a pan until fully cooked.
  5. Assemble the sliders with the beef patties, cheese, and any other toppings you like.
  6. *Optional: Chop up onions and caramelize them for more flavor in your sliders!
  7. Enjoy your delicious homemade sliders!
